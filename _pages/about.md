# Junteng Liu

First-year PhD candidate at [HKUST NLP Group](https://junxianhe.github.io/nlp/).

Graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2024.

Research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning.

Research interests include LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning, Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM), LLM truthfulness and Interpretability.
